About



I am a people's person. I love humans. And I especially love talking to them. Be it in person or through the alphabets on the screen. That's why I have had the most fun while working as a business developer for past 6 years.

It was a short video teaching python basics, but since then I have been hooked. It wasn't an on the spot decision to move away from what I loved as a profession. However, what started as a side hobby, soon turned into an obssession- a good kind of obssession.
